Data Scientist

Company Overview

We are looking for a highly motivated Data Scientist to join our analytics team. The candidate will work on machine learning, predictive analytics, data visualization, and AI-driven business solutions.

Responsibilities

* Analyze large datasets to extract meaningful insights.
* Build predictive analytics solutions.
* Design and deploy NLP applications.
* Create dashboards and visualizations for business stakeholders.
* Collaborate with software engineers and business teams.
* Perform feature engineering and model optimization.
